| 1 | Accessibility | HeroSection.tsx, AboutSection.tsx | Both files | High | No `prefers-reduced-motion` handling. | 2 | Responsive Design | AboutSection.tsx | Line 33 | Medium | Grid jumps from `grid-cols-1` directly to `md:grid-cols-3`.
